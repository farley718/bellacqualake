Bell Acqua Lake — Waiver & Release of Liability

Please read and sign the complete waiver below.

Bell Acqua, Inc. ("Bell Acqua") owns the property at 935 E Street, Rio Linda CA ("Property"). Bell Acqua and its Shareholders ("Shareholders") require any participants and guests on the property to agree to certain conditions and acknowledge the risks associated with water-skiing and other activities, which may be carried out on the property. Please read the following and if you agree, sign below. If you do not agree to the following and do not sign this waiver of liability, you will not be permitted to water-ski on the property or participate in any associated activities on the property.


As a participant of water-sports or any other activity on the property. I acknowledge and fully understand that I will be engaging in activities that involve risk of serious injury, including permanent disability and death, and associated social and economic losses, which might result not only from my own actions or negligence but also the actions or negligence of others, or the conditions of the premises or any equipment used.


I agree to inspect the facilities and equipment to be used and if I believe anything is unsafe, I will immediately advise a shareholder of Bell Acqua and refuse to participate.


I assume all risk and accept personal responsibility for the damages following an injury, permanent disability, or death.


I release Bell Acqua, its shareholders, guests and/or employees of any liability for any and all claims, demands, losses or damages on the account of injury, including death or damage to property, caused or alleged to be caused in whole or in part by the negligence of Bell Acqua, its shareholders, guests and/or employees. I acknowledge that the rules of the property have been explained to me and that I understand them.


I acknowledge that, when wake-boarding, swimming, water ski jumping, water-skiing, knee-boarding, bare-footing or being towed behind any boat, I must stay at least 25 feet from the shore at all times. When I am traveling around the islands, I will always follow the boat's path and never travel outside the boat's wake. I the skier must stay in the center of the boat wake while boat is turning around the islands. I must know the established set of signals to indicate a change in direction, a change in speed, or that a skier is not hurt after a fall.


By my signature below, I acknowledge that water-skiing is an inherently dangerous sport; that I am responsible for my safety; I will obey the rules and regulations posted and verbally communicated to me by shareholders of Bell Acqua; any injury I may suffer while participating in any activity on the property owned by Bell Acqua, is solely my responsibility.

Section A — Express Assumption of All Inherent Risks of Water Sports Activities

I, the undersigned, am aware that water-skiing, wake-boarding, and all other water sports activities involve inherent risks of injury or death. I voluntarily and expressly assume all risks associated with participation in water sports activities at Bell Acqua Lake, including but not limited to: drowning, collision with other participants or watercraft, falls and related injuries, equipment failure, variations in water and weather conditions, and any other risk whether foreseen or unforeseen.

I understand that Bell Acqua Lake, its shareholders, employees, and agents are not responsible for any injury, loss, or damage incurred as a result of my participation, including injury caused by the negligence of Bell Acqua Lake or any other party.

Section B — Indemnity Agreement Statement

I agree to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Bell Acqua, Inc., its shareholders, officers, employees, agents, and assigns from and against any and all claims, actions, damages, liability, and expense in connection with loss of life, personal injury, or damage to property arising from or out of any occurrence in, upon, or at the property, or the occupancy or use by me of the property or any part thereof. This indemnification shall include the payment of reasonable attorney's fees and costs incurred by Bell Acqua in defending such claims.

Section C — Waiver and Release of Liability

In consideration for being permitted to participate in activities at Bell Acqua Lake, I hereby release, waive, discharge, and covenant not to sue Bell Acqua, Inc., its shareholders, directors, officers, employees, volunteers, agents, and assigns (collectively "Releasees") from any and all liability, claims, demands, actions, and causes of action whatsoever arising out of or related to any loss, damage, or injury, including death, that may be sustained by me or to any property belonging to me, while participating in any activity at the property, or while in, on, or upon the premises, WHETHER CAUSED BY THE NEGLIGENCE OF THE RELEASEES, or otherwise.

This waiver and release shall be construed broadly to provide a release and waiver to the maximum extent permissible under applicable law.

Section D — Declaration of Competency and Intent to Be Bound

I declare that I am of sound mind and competent to enter into this agreement. I am at least 18 years of age, or if under 18 years of age, I have the consent of my parent or guardian who has also signed this waiver. I am voluntarily participating in activities at Bell Acqua Lake with knowledge of the risks involved.

I intend this release to be a complete and unconditional release of all liability to the greatest extent allowed by law. I agree that if any portion of this agreement is found to be void or unenforceable, the remaining portions shall remain in full force and effect.

I have read this entire agreement and I fully understand its terms. I understand that I am giving up substantial rights by signing it. I acknowledge that I am signing freely and voluntarily, and intend my signature to be a complete and unconditional release of all liability to the greatest extent allowed by law.

Section E — Unconditional General Release from Liability

I, for myself, my heirs, personal representatives, or assigns, do hereby release, waive, discharge and covenant to hold harmless Bell Acqua, Inc. and its shareholders from liability from any and all claims including the negligence of Bell Acqua, Inc. and its shareholders, resulting in personal injury, accidents or illnesses (including death), and property loss arising from, but not limited to, participation in water sports and recreational activities at the property located at 935 E Street, Rio Linda, CA.
