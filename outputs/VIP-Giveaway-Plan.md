# Bell Acqua Lake — VIP Membership Giveaway Campaign

This plan outlines the copy strategy and deliverables for the new June Facebook Ad campaign. The goal is to generate leads by offering a chance to win a 2-Week VIP Membership (Value: $264). 

## User Review Required

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Prize Clarification:** The prize is described as "ski unlimited for two weeks" OR "ski for two weeks with VIP rate at 70% off ($23 per run)". Since it's a giveaway, usually the prize is free unlimited skiing, and the *runner-up* or *consolation* offer is the 70% off. Let me know if you want to use the 70% off offer as a "consolation prize" for everyone who doesn't win the grand prize (this is a great way to monetize the leads immediately!).
>
> **Offer Constraints:** We will add clear disclaimers that the winner *must already know how to ski* (no first-timers). 

## Open Questions

> [!WARNING]
> 1. **Runner-Up Offer:** Should the 3-email sequence immediately offer them the $23/run VIP rate just for entering, or should we wait until June 30th when the winner is announced to offer it to the non-winners?
> 2. **Form Fields:** Are we capturing any "experience level" on the landing page form to filter out first-timers, or just stating it in the copy and capturing Name, Email, and Phone?

## Proposed Deliverables (Copy Strategy)

### 1. Landing Page Copy
- **Headline:** Focus on the ultimate water skiing experience (Driver, Coach, VIP Access).
- **Subheadline:** State the value ($264) and urgency (Winner drawn June 30).
- **Body/Bullets:** Highlight the perks (unlimited skiing for 2 weeks, dedicated driver and coach).
- **Disclaimers:** "Must not be a first-timer. Must know how to ski."
- **Call to Action (Form):** Name, Email, Phone Number.

### 2. Post-Signup Email Sequence (3 Emails)
- **Email 1 (Immediate):** Confirmation of entry. Remind them of the draw date (June 30). Set expectations.
- **Email 2 (Mid-June):** Build excitement. Highlight the coaching and driver perks. 
- **Email 3 (June 30 / Winner Announcement):** Announce the winner. **Strategy recommendation:** Offer all non-winners the 70% off VIP rate ($23/ride) as a consolation prize to drive immediate revenue.

### 3. Post-Signup SMS (2 SMS)
- **SMS 1 (Immediate):** Quick confirmation. "You're entered to win the Bell Acqua VIP Membership! We'll announce the winner via email on June 30. Good luck!"
- **SMS 2 (June 30):** Winner announcement alert. "The Bell Acqua VIP winner has been drawn! Check your email to see if you won (and to claim your runner-up gift!)."
